About the C10 Index

Index Definition

The C10 Index tracks the performance of the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization, using a market-cap weighted methodology to provide diversified digital asset exposure.

Weight Rules

Market-cap based weight allocation with a maximum single asset weight of 50%, ensuring index diversification and risk distribution.

Mature Methodology

Employs proven index construction methods with transparent rules and rigorous governance processes, ensuring index reliability and consistency.

Regular Rebalancing
