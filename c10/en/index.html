FFAI Crypto Treasury Strategy

Corporate-Level Crypto Asset Treasury Strategy

The FFAI Crypto Treasury is operated independently by a wholly owned subsidiary. It uses the FFAI C10 Index as a passive core position (with any single asset capped at 50% weight) and supplements it with 20% actively managed positions. Within a framework of compliant custody and staking yield generation, the strategy involves raising capital in stages to acquire and hold the top ten mainstream cryptocurrencies by circulating market capitalization. Multiple downside-protection mechanisms are built in to stabilize cash flow and enhance net assets. This corporate-level digital asset treasury is designed to work in synergy with the EAI EV Flywheel, creating mutual reinforcement between the two growth engines.

Core Strategy

  • Passive core portfolio based on the C10 Index
  • Maximum 50% allocation to any single cryptocurrency
  • 20% allocation to actively managed positions

Operating Framework

  • Managed independently by a wholly owned subsidiary
  • Compliant custody with staking yield enhancement
  • Layered downside-protection mechanisms

Strategic Objectives

  • Maintain stable cash flow
  • Strengthen net asset value
  • Drive mutual growth with the EAI EV Flywheel

Key indicators

Technology & Security

Providing institutional-grade protection and optimization for digital assets through multi-layered security architecture, dynamic risk management, and comprehensive compliance systems.

Multi-tier Cold/Hot Wallet Separation & Multi-Sig

Large assets stored long-term in offline cold wallets to reduce hacking risks
Hot wallets used only for necessary liquidity operations with multi-signature approval processes

Dynamic Leverage & Debt Management

Following MicroStrategy model, increase holdings through low-cost convertible bonds or targeted loans during high market valuations

Regular Stress Testing & Scenario Simulation
