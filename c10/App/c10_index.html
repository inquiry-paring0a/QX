About the C10 Index

Index Definition

Centered on Bitcoin and Ethereum, encompassing the top ten crypto assets by circulating market cap excluding stablecoins, calculated using market cap weighted average rules.

Weighting Rules

Single asset weight capped at 50%; periodic rebalancing restores target weights; constituents must meet liquidity and eligibility screening standards.

Proven Methodology

Our index algorithm references the mature methodologies of NASDAQ and S&P 500, ensuring scientific rigor while maintaining sufficient transparency and traceability.

Periodic Rebalancing

Monthly weight adjustments ensure the index composition always reflects the latest market conditions and circulating market cap rankings.
