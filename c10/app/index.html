C10 Treasury Portfolio Management

The FFAI Crypto Treasury is the core business of FFAI’s digital asset treasury strategy. Its inaugural product, the C10 Treasury, is dynamically allocated based on the C10 Index—a portfolio of the world’s top ten cryptocurrencies by market capitalization. The strategy aims to grow assets through professional, compliant custody, staking yield from locked holdings, and portfolio compounding, while multiple downside-protection mechanisms help manage risk. Together, these features support stable cash flow, optimize net asset quality, and drive sustained growth in company value.

Core Strategy

  • 80% passive allocation, strictly based on C10 index
  • 20% active allocation, actively managed

Operating Framework

  • Managed independently by a wholly owned subsidiary
  • Compliant custody with staking yield
  • Multiple downside-protection mechanisms

Key indicators

  • AFY (Annual Fixed Yield)
  • FAY (Find Annual Yield)
  • CAVPS (Crypto Asset Value per Share)

Why C10 Index?

  • Alignment with the industry’s long-term bull market through a diversified basket approach
  • Reduction of single-token risk with a multi-asset index portfolio
  • An effortless, efficient way to participate in the growth of the crypto industry

C10 Index: Key Definition

The first basket index of the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization (excluding stablecoins), initiated by a U.S.-listed company. Calculated based on market-cap weighting and other rules, with the weight of any single asset capped at 50%.

Technology & Security

Providing institutional-grade protection and optimization for digital assets through multi-layered security architecture, dynamic risk management, and comprehensive compliance systems.

Multi-tier Cold & Hot Wallet Separation with Multi-Signature Security

Large assets stored long-term in offline cold wallets to reduce hacking risks
Hot wallets used only for necessary liquidity operations with multi-signature approval processes

Dynamic Leverage & Debt Management

Similar to the MicroStrategy approach, during high market valuation periods, increase holdings via low-cost convertible bonds or private loans; during low valuation periods, optimize the balance sheet through partial asset liquidation or debt buybacks.

Regular Stress Testing & Scenario Simulation

The portfolio undergoes quarterly stress tests, covering scenarios like extreme price volatility, liquidity shortages, and exchange risks. Contingency plans are prepared in advance to respond to potential risks.

Compliance & Tax Optimization

Ongoing monitoring and adherence to digital asset regulations in relevant jurisdictions, with tax and compliance optimization achieved, where appropriate, through trusts or offshore Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs).
