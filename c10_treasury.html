Section 1 · Overview

C10 Treasury operates a dual‑engine model of passive index + active sleeve:

  • Passive (C10): Rules‑based tracking of the free‑float market cap top 10 crypto assets with scheduled rebalances; objective: market beta.
  • Active (C0): 20% sleeve for event‑driven opportunities, correlation trades, hedging, and liquidity management; objective: improve drawdowns and Sharpe.
  • Full‑stack compliance and security: MPC custody, tiered permissions, allowlists, on‑chain monitoring, and audit logs.

Process Overview

  1. Strategy design and risk budgeting
  2. Create transaction intent in the console and run compliance checks
  3. MPC signing and tiered cold/warm fund movements
  4. Execution, settlement, and reconciliation
  5. Weekly reporting and public disclosures
