About the C10 Index

  1. Definition. A free‑float, market‑cap weighted basket of the ten largest crypto assets. Stablecoins may be included where relevant to market structure.
  2. Weighting Rules. Caps and floors applied to mitigate concentration; scheduled rebalances restore target weights; constituents must meet liquidity and eligibility screens.
  3. Trend View. The chart (right) shows historical levels of the index based on the published methodology.
  4. Constituent List. The current top‑10 assets with symbols, names, prices, market caps and 24h change are disclosed and updated regularly.
